TCMG 412: Contemporary Issues in TCMG

TCMG 412 is a course about DevOps and the Modern IT team. This course designed to give students the necessary tools to operate in the modern day IT field. The course provides students the opportunity to work in teams to deliver modern IT services and experience the DevOps model of service. The goal is to give students a basic understanding of what the DevOps model of IT service delivery is like. Students will also acquire hands-on experience through a number of commonly used tools in the DevOps environment.
